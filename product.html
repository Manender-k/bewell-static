BeWell CXR-TB

AI to detect Tuberculosis and interpret Chest X-rays

Product features at a glance:

  • Detects the likely presence of Tuberculosis in a matter of minutes
  • Differentiates between normal and abnormal X-rays to reduce the radiologist’s worklist
  • Provides a score in the range of 0 to 100 indicating the presence of active TB.
  • Helps programs to conduct mass screening for Tuberculosis and also optimize the subsequent use of Sputum test.
  • Drastically reduces the time taken to provide treatment

“Product is currently under development and is yet to be clinically validated’

BeWell MGram

AI to detect Breast Cancer in screening mammograms

Product features at a glance:

  • Mammographers read and diagnose mammograms faster
  • Provides an accurate reading of suspicion lesions indicative of cancer
  • AI findings are highlighted to aid Mammographers and enable them to read mammograms faster
  • An automated report with the findings is generated within a few minutes

BeWell DiaRet

AI software for detection of diabetic retinopathy

Product features at a glance:

  • Autonomously detects Diabetic retinopathy
  • Accurately detect referable diabetic retinopathy patients
  • Automated report is generated within a few minutes
